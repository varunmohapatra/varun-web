Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Jan 20, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect basic information such as your name and email address when you interact with the app. This information is used solely for providing a better experience. No sign-in is required to use the app.

2. User Preferences and Data Storage

All user settings and preferences, such as favorite teachers and notification preferences, are stored locally on your device. We do not store any of this data on our servers. Basic app usage data may be collected by Apple as part of their standard app tracking.

3. Notifications

We ask for your consent before sending notifications. You can choose to receive daily wisdom quotes and manage your preferences within the app.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party services for analytics, advertising, or other purposes. Our app only utilizes the Apple App Store for app distribution and secure purchases.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We do not store personal data on our servers, and everything is kept locally on your device. Since no personal data is stored with us, there is no need for deletion requests.

6. Security

We rely on Apple’s secure purchase methods for any transactions made within the app, and your data is protected by Apple's security protocols.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our app may be used by children, and we do not collect any extra personal information from users under the age of 13. Settings and preferences are stored on the user's device same as anyone else.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we ever make changes to this privacy policy, the updated version will be available within the app, and you will be notified accordingly.
